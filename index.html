Scientific information:

  • Procyon is a genus of nocturnal mammals, comprising three species commonly known as raccoons
  • The most familiar species, the common raccoon, as the two other raccoon species in the genus are native only to the tropics and less well known
  • Classification:
    • Kingdom: Animalia
    • Phylum: Chordata
    • Class: Mammalia
    • Order: Carnivora
    • Family: Procyonidae
    • Genus: Procyon
